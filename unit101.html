Unit 1 - Outcome 1


1.1 - Describe the purpose of IT in modern business

The purpose of IT in modern business is to improve business processes and planning. For example, a fashion brand needs an efficient platform to market itself and increase sales. Good resource management helps with product distribution and customer support, which results in increased value, enhanced quality and boosts productivity. Other benefits include, higher global sales, systematic management and real time monitoring. Innovation results in smarter apps, improved data storage, faster processing and wider information distribution.
